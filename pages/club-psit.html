About Us

PSIT - The Physics Society of IISER - Thiruvananthapuram is a student-run club under the guidance of School of Physics whose sole purpose is to share the passion we hold for Physics. From informative content over on Instagram, regular peer discussions on thought-provoking topics, and organising talks from established researchers to making mini rockets and showcasing the concept of quantum levitation experimentally, PSI(t) is an action-packed club where the love for Physics only amplifies as time passes.
