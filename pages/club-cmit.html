About Us

CMIT, the Club of Mathematics IISER Thiruvananthapuram, is a student-driven initiative under the School of Mathematics (SoM) that fosters mathematical exploration and appreciation through engaging activities like talks, discussions, games, and competitions. Our initiatives include Tangent Tuesday and Talk series, as well as themed events such as S^n, our annual foundation day celebration featuring various programs like talks and outreach activities during the Varsha semester, and Pi week, a celebration of mathematical constant with competitions and a treasure hunt (Epiphany) during the Vasanth semester. We also publish 'Donut', the magazine showcasing mathematical insights, events, and creative works.
